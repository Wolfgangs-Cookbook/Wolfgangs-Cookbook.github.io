About Wolfgang's Cookbook

Wolfgang's Cookbook was created to offer high-flavor recipes that respect modern health needs. Inspired by the FDA’s recent recommendations to reduce sodium intake, the site focuses on meals that taste great while going easier on salt, sugar, and processed ingredients. Many recipes are also designed to be low-carb or IBS-friendly. Whether you’re cooking to manage a condition or just eat better day-to-day, the goal here is to keep cooking joyful without sacrificing wellness.

Meet the Chef

Hi, I’m Wolfgang Ofstedal — I live in Miami, Florida but grew up in the Midwest. Cooking has always been my creative outlet, and over time it’s also become a personal path to better health. I was diagnosed with a rare kidney condition called FSGS (Focal Segmental Glomerulosclerosis), which means I have to be especially mindful about salt in my diet. That challenge pushed me to explore new techniques and cuisines that could satisfy without overloading on sodium. This site is the result of that journey — a space to share what I’m learning and loving in the kitchen.
