About Wolfgang's Cookbook

Wolfgang's Cookbook was created to offer high-flavor recipes that are as healthy as possible. Inspired by my personal sodium restriction, which seems to be a more and more common practice. The Average daily sodium intake in the U.S. is approximately 3,400mg/day, while the FDA guidelines for adults is only 2,300mg/day. The goal of these recipes is totaste great while going easier on salt, sugar, and processed ingredients. Whether you’re cooking to manage a healthcondition or just want to eat better day-to-day, the goal here is to keep cooking joyful without sacrificing wellness.

Meet the Chef

I’m Wolfgang Ofstedal and I live in Miami, Florida but grew up in the Midwest. Cooking is part of my daily routine in order to stay alive, and over time it’s also become a an easy way to stay healthy and avoid the highly salty, over-sweetened, and over-processed foods that are so common in restaurants and prepared foods. I was diagnosed with a rare kidney condition at a young age, which means I have to be especially mindful about salt in my diet. I also aspire to live forever. These goals have pushed me to explore new techniques and recipes that can satisfy without sacrificing health. This site is a simple collection of recipes that I've found and developed over the years.
