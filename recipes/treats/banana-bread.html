Classic Banana Bread

Moist, Not-Too-Sweet Quick Bread

Ingredients

  • 3-4 very ripe bananas, mashed (about 1½ cups)
  • ⅓ cup melted butter
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅔ cup brown sugar (packed)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • Optional: ½ cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix mashed bananas with melted butter and yogurt.
  3. Beat in eggs, then stir in brown sugar and vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  5. Fold dry ingredients into wet mixture until just combined. Don't overmix.
  6. If using nuts, fold them in gently.
  7. Pour into prepared pan, smooth top.
  8. Bake 55-65 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Cool in pan 10 minutes, then remove to wire rack.

Tips

  • Use overripe bananas with black spots for best flavor
  • Yogurt can be replaced with sour cream
  • Don't overmix - some small lumps are okay
  • Freezes well for up to 3 months
