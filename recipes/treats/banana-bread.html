Classic Banana Bread

Moist, Not-Too-Sweet Quick Bread

Ingredients

  • 3-4 very ripe bananas
  • 3/4 cup softened butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅔ cup brown sugar (packed)
  • 1.5 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Optional: ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • Optional: ½ cup chopped walnuts or pecans
  • Optional: ½ cup chocolate chips
  • Optional: 1/2 cup yogurt orsour cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk softened butter and brown sugar until creamy.
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  5. If using yogurt or sour cream, mix it in with the wet ingredients.
  6. Fold dry ingredients into wet mixture until just combined. Don't overmix.
  7. If using nuts or chocolate chips, fold them in gently.
  8. Lightly mash / fold bananas into batter.
  9. Pour into prepared pan, smooth top, indenting the middle to prevent it from rising too high.
  10. Bake 50-60 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  11. Cool in pan 10 minutes, then remove to wire rack.

Tips

  • Use overripe bananas with black spots for best flavor
  • Don't overmix - some small lumps are okay
  • Freezes well for up to 3 months
