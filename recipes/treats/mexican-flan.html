Mexican Flan

Silky Smooth Caramel Custard

Ingredients

  • 1 cup granulated sugar (for caramel)
  • 1 14oz can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 12oz can evaporated milk
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • ¬º teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325¬∞F (165¬∞C). Have ready a 9-inch cake pan and a larger baking dish for the water bath.
  2. Make the caramel: Heat sugar in a saucepan over medium heat, stirring constantly until it melts and turns golden amber (about 8-10 minutes). Immediately pour into cake pan, swirling to coat bottom.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together condensed milk, evaporated milk, whole milk, eggs, egg yolks, vanilla, and salt until smooth.
  4. Strain mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into the caramel-lined pan.
  5. Place cake pan in larger baking dish. Fill outer dish with hot water halfway up the sides of the flan pan.
  6. Bake for 50-60 minutes until center is just set but still jiggly (like gelatin).
  7. Remove from water bath and cool completely on a wire rack.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  9. To serve, run a knife around the edge and invert onto a rimmed serving plate.

Tips

  • Watch the caramel closely as it can burn quickly
  • Straining ensures silky smooth texture
  • Don't overbake - flan should be barely set when done
  • Can be made up to 3 days ahead
