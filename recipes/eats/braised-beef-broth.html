Braised Beef Broth

Deep, rich broth from roasted beef bones

Ingredients

  • 3–4 lbs beef bones (ideally with some meat attached)
  • Filtered water (enough to cover in pot)

Instructions

  1. Blanch bones in boiling water for 1 minute, blanching in small batches to prevent water from losing heat.
  2. Drain and cool bones.Discard water.
  3. Preheat oven to 370°F (190°C). Place bones in single layer on baking tray.
  4. Braise in oven for 25 minutes, flipping halfway through until deeply browned.
  5. Transfer bones to a large stockpot. Add enough filtered water to cover bones.
  6. Bring to a gentle simmer. Skim off any foam or fat.
  7. Simmer uncovered for 8–10 hours, replenishing water as needed.
  8. Let cool. Skim fat if desired.

Tips

  • For a quicker method, use an Instant Pot on high pressure for 45-60 minutes.
  • Freeze in portions for soups, sauces, or sipping warm.
