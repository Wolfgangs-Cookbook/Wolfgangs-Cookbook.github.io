"Chipotle"-Style Chicken Marinade

Smoky, citrusy, and bold. Perfect for burrito bowls or tacos

Note that this recipe is unfinished and needs to be updated

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice (about 2 limes)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons chipotle chili powder (or use adobo paste if available)
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine all ingredients except chicken. Mix well.
  2. Add chicken thighs and coat evenly in marinade.
  3. Cover and marinate at least 1 hour, ideally 8–24 hours in the fridge.
  4. Grill or sear over medium-high heat until cooked through and lightly charred.
  5. Let rest 5 minutes before slicing. Serve warm.

Tips

  • For more heat, add chopped chipotle peppers in adobo sauce.
  • Pairs well with rice bowls, tacos, salads, or even as a protein prep.
  • Leftovers keep well for 3–4 days in the fridge.
