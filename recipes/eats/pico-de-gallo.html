Fresh Pico de Gallo

Simple, zesty Mexican-style fresh salsa

Ingredients

  • 7 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 2 jalapeños, finely diced
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • Optional: 1 white onion, finely diced
  • Optional: 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions

  1. Dice tomatoes into small, uniform cubes (about 1/4 inch)
  2. Finely dice jalapeños (remove seeds for less heat)
  3. Combine tomatoes and jalapeños in a large bowl
  4. Add fresh lime juice
  5. Sprinkle with salt
  6. Gently toss all ingredients
  7. Let rest 10 minutes to allow flavors to meld
  8. Taste and adjust salt if needed

Tips

  • Use firm, ripe tomatoes for best texture
  • Can be made up to 4 hours ahead
  • Add onion and cilantro for traditional style
  • Serves 6 generously
