Norwegian Pancakes

Thin, soft, and perfect for sweet or savory fillings

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • Butter or oil for the pan

Instructions

  1. Whisk together eggs and milk in a medium bowl.
  2. Add flour, sugar, and salt. Whisk until smooth and lump-free.
  3. Let batter rest 10–15 minutes (or refrigerate up to overnight).
  4. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium. Lightly grease with butter or oil.
  5. Pour in about ¼ cup of batter. Tilt pan to spread it thin and evenly.
  6. Cook 1–2 minutes until edges lift and bottom is golden.
  7. Flip and cook another 30–60 seconds. Repeat with remaining batter.

Tips

  • Serve rolled with jam, sugar, berries, or savory fillings.
  • Can be made ahead and stored in the fridge for 2–3 days.
  • Traditionally eaten as a light lunch or dessert in Norway.
