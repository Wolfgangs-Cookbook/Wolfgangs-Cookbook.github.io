Baked Baby Back Ribs

Tender, fall-off-the-bone ribs with a caramelized BBQ finish

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 rack baby back ribs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup dry rub seasoning
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce
  • Heavy duty aluminum foil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F (150°C)
  2. Check back of ribs for membrane, remove if present by sliding knife under and peeling off
  3. Pat ribs dry with paper towels
  4. Rub olive oil over entire surface of ribs
  5. Apply dry rub generously to both sides, pressing into meat
  6. Wrap ribs tightly in aluminum foil, sealing well to prevent leaks
  7. Place wrapped ribs on baking sheet, meat side down
  8. Bake for 3.5 hours until nearlytender
  9. Remove from oven (watch for hot grease drips)and increase temperature to 350°F (175°C)
  10. Carefully unwrap ribs (watch for hot steam)
  11. Brush generously with BBQ sauce
  12. Return unwrapped to oven for 30 minutes until sauce is caramelized
  13. Let rest 10 minutes before cutting

Tips

  • For extra tender ribs, bake up to 4 hours at 300°F
  • Test doneness by twisting a bone - it should move easily
  • Once meat falls from the bone undisturbed, it's overcooked
  • Apply sauce in 2-3 thin layers during final baking
  • For spicier ribs, add cayenne to your dry rub
