Classic Lasagna

Layers of pasta, meat sauce, and three cheeses

Note that this recipe is unfinished and needs to be updated

Ingredients

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1.5 lbs ground beef
  • 2 (28oz) cans crushed tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 16 oz mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs (for ricotta mixture)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp Italian herbs

Instructions

  1. Boil lasagna noodles until nearly al dente (about 2 minutes less than package directions). Drain and set aside.
  2. Brown ground beef in a large pot over medium-high heat. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add diced onion and bell pepper to the meat, cook until softened.
  4. Add garlic, cook 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Pour in crushed tomatoes, herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer 20 minutes.
  6. Mix ricotta with eggs and 1/2 cup parmesan cheese.
  7. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  8. Layer in 9x13 pan:
    • Thin layer of sauce
    • Noodles
    • Ricotta mixture
    • Meat sauce
    • Mozzarella
    • Repeat layers 2-3 times
  9. Top final layer with remaining mozzarella and parmesan.
  10. Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes.
  11. Remove foil and broil on medium-high for 3 minutes until cheese is golden.
  12. Let rest 15 minutes before serving.

Tips

  • Can be assembled ahead and refrigerated up to 24 hours
  • Freeze unbaked for up to 3 months
  • Use fresh mozzarella for top layer for best browning
