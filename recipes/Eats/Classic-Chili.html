Classic Chili

Hearty American Bean & Meat Chili

Ingredients

  • 1.5 to 2lbs Ground Meat (Combination of Beef and Pork)
  • 1 Large Sweet Yellow Onion, diced
  • 1 Green or Red Bell Pepper, diced
  • 4 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 1 28oz Can Tomatoes (Whole or Diced)
  • 3 15.5oz Cans of Beans (Kidney, Black, Pinto, Great Northern or Cannellini, or Chili Beans of choice)
  • 3 tbsp Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 can any beer (optional)
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)

Instructions

  1. Begin browning meat in pan.
  2. Add garlic and diced onion after 1 minute.
  3. Add diced bell pepper after 4 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes, cover, and bring to a simmer.
  5. Mash tomatoes if whole, and simmer covered for 10 minutes.
  6. Add beans, cumin, chili powder, and beer or wine if using.
  7. Cover and simmer for 30-45 minutes. Add salt and black pepper to flavor.

Tips

  • Refrigerate overnight for better flavor.
