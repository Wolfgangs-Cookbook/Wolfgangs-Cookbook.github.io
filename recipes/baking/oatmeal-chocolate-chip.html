Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

A soft, chewy, naturally flavorful cookie with whole oats and less sugar. Sweetened just enough, with dark chocolate in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or less if using salted butter)
  • 3 cups old-fashioned oats
  • 1 1/2 cups dark chocolate chips or chunks

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla. Beat until combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir into wet ingredients.
  5. Mix in oats and chocolate chips until evenly distributed.
  6. Scoop tablespoon-sized dough balls onto baking sheet, spaced 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake 10–12 minutes or until edges are golden brown. Centers may look slightly underbaked — that’s perfect.
  8. Cool on tray for 5 mins, then move to a wire rack.
