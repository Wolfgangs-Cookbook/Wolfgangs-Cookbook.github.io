No-Cook San Marzano Pizza Sauce

Simple, authentic Neapolitan-style sauce, low-sodium

Ingredients

  • 1 (28oz) can whole San Marzano tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Optional: 1/4 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions

  1. Drain excess liquid from the tomatoes, reserving if needed for consistency.
  2. Hand-crush the whole tomatoes into a bowl, removing any tough stem pieces.
  3. Add olive oil and salt, stir gently to combine.
  4. Let rest at room temperature 30 minutes before using.

Tips

  • Use DOP certified San Marzano tomatoes for best results
  • Don't blend - the rough texture is ideal for pizza
  • Can be stored refrigerated up to 5 days, or frozen for up to 3 months
  • Makes enough for 6-8 pizzas
