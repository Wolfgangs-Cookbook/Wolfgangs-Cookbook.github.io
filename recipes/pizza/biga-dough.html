Low-Salt Biga Neapolitan Dough

Classic 65% Hydration with an Overnight Biga Preferment

Note that this recipe is unfinished and needs to be updated

  • Biga is a traditional Italian pre-ferment made with flour, yeast, and water, that can be used in many breads.
  • Biga is similar to Poolish, another Italian pre-ferment, but biga is made with a lower hydration and is fermented for longer.Generally, biga will be heavier and more dense than a poolish, with bulk ferment being the densest.
  • Regardless of whether you use bulk fermentation, biga, or poolish, you will likely need to use trial and error to find the best ingredient ratios because every flour, yeast, and oven are different, and also elevation could play a factor.
  • The below recipe is simply what I found to work well for me.
