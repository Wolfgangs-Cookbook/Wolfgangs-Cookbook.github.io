Low-Salt Neapolitan Dough

12–72hr Refrigerated Bulk-Ferment

Ingredients

  • 500g 00 flour (or bread flour)
  • 325g water (65%)
  • 2g instant yeast
  • 7g salt

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Let rest 20 minutes (autolyse).
  2. Knead gently for 5–8 minutes until smooth. Cover and let rise at room temp for 2–3 hours.
  3. Divide into 2–4 dough balls. Place in lightly oiled containers.
  4. Cold ferment in the fridge for 24–48 hours.
  5. Let dough warm to room temp for 1–2 hours before stretching.
  6. Bake at 500–550°F on a pizza steel or stone until bubbly and charred (6–8 minutes).

Tips

  • Use the dough within 72 hours max.
  • For more tang, extend the cold ferment to 72 hours (reduce yeast slightly).
